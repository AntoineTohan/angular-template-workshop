Présentation

Preserver notre planete avec des gestes simples

we are Terra Salvare. we have smokers in our team, and they can't say that they never thrown a cigarette butt in the street. But nonetheless, starting little by little, we are striving for a better world. A world that will not consume the next generations. with your help, we can stop the increase of water pollution, help trees regain their own, legitimate kingdom, and put a stop to the chemical waste that is poisoning & killing our environment. All these aspects of our human world are leading to a simple solution : Terra Salvare, or if you will, the power of a united force. A force helping each other. The time to rely on others is over. We are already in debt to the planet, all of us whether we like it or not. Simply by putting the end of your cigarettes, or your lighters into our odor control boxes, boxes specially made for this purpose, and from recycled materials - by doing this, you can make a difference. The process is child's play, you send us your boxes, bought on our website, with our special postage stamp so that you won't have to pay for the shipping. Then, we will recycle them, and make very special products purchasable on our website, or, if you prefer, you can give some of your points or some of your income... to an association which is actively fighting against the decay of our world. make our world cleaner with terra salvare...
